About Us

Who are we

  • Christian awareness initiative of Nigerian (CHAIN),a christian non-governmental organization.
  • As depicted by our acronym CHAIN, which interlinks and binds, our aims and objective are;
  • Promoting and educating Christians in all spheres of human endeavours.
  • Raising up Godly Christians morally, socially and educationally.
  • Equipping the society with Christian teachings.
  • Sensitizing Christians to the importance of voluntary service towards community development.
  • Educate Christians on health, environment, cultural, political, social, professional, inter and intra religious issues by organizing workshops, seminars, conferences and trainings.
  • Creating awareness on the evils of women and child abuses, danger of maternal and child mortality, trafficking in humans and general apathy to civic responsibilities.
  • Assisting the indigent people in the church and the society at large.
  • Encouraging society achievers and organizations to contribute meaningfully towards the development of Christianity in our society.
  • Partnering with government at the federal, state and local government levels in Nigeria towards achieving the set out millennium development goals.
  • Partnering with the three tiers of government and all relevant stakeholders in achieving sustainable peace in the Nigerian society.
  • Offering Godly, unbiased and untainted advices and criticisms to the three tiers of government in Nigeria as tokens to averting, forestalling and arresting civil/religious unrests as pre-emptive measure in safe guarding lives and properties of the general Nigerian citizenry.
  • Educating local and international communities of the true picture of political, ethnic, religious or any other happenings within Nigerian without bias and or falsehood
  • Soliciting for Christian prayers and understanding especially of the sectarian persecution of Christians in Nigerian and support for Christian victims in the advent of losses of lives and properties.
  • Soliciting for instructional/logistical materials or equipments and other aids for churches and other Christian organization to support them in their teachings and evangelism.

Our vision

  • To continually awaken and ginger up Christians in Nigeria to their spiritual and civic responsibilities to God and the society.
  • Arrest and re-channel Christian resources both materials and human from been wasted on destructive worldly vices to the most edifying service to God and humanity in Nigerian.
  • Entrench awareness as an indispensible companion of Christians in their daily lives.
  • God's people perish or are destroyed for lack knowledge (Hosea 4:6). So continual trainings and refreshment in the knowledge of the things of God as they relate to the spirituality of individuals and society are tasks the CHAIN is poised to achieve.
  • Strive to eliminate or minimize ignorance from within the Christian mould
  • Promote tolerance and understanding among ethnic and religious groups.
  • As the world is full of vile moral vices; we shall stand for the truth; honesty, courage, accountability and braveness.

Strategy

  • To enable us achieve our set out goals and objectives, we shall adopt various methods in executing our programs. Such methods include;
  • Outright teaching in churches, Christian gatherings, schools E.t.c
  • Organizing of seminars, workshops, conferences and summits
  • Use of audio and visual instructional materials and reach out.
  • Use of the electronic and print media to highlight the Christians' position(s)on various matters as need be.
